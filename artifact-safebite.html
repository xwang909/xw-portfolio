Artifact 01

SafeBite — AI-Powered Food Safety and Dietary Assistant

A user-centered AI concept that helps people evaluate food products more safely by reading ingredient labels, recognizing allergens and dietary concerns, and responding with concise safety guidance.

Back to Artifacts Jump to Evidence

Overview

SafeBite is a practical AI prototype designed to help users make faster and safer food decisions. It interprets packaging information such as ingredient labels and nutrition facts, then translates that information into a simple recommendation based on allergies and dietary needs.

Core Features

  • Identifies common allergens
  • Checks gluten-free and lactose-free concerns
  • Flags sugar level concerns
  • Responds with a quick Safe / Not Safe decision

Objective

The goal of SafeBite was to create a user-centered AI assistant that supports real shopping decisions by making food label interpretation faster, clearer, and more personalized.

How SafeBite Works

Step 1

Input

Food label image, nutrition panel, or ingredient text

→
Step 2

AI Processing

Ingredient parsing, allergen detection, and dietary rule checking

Step 3

Output

"Safe Bite" or "Not Safe Bite" with a short explanation

Decision Logic Example

Contains milk

Not safe for lactose intolerance

Shared with peanuts

Not safe because of cross-contamination risk

High added sugar

Marked as a caution even if major allergens are absent
