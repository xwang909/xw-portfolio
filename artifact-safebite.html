Artifact 01

SafeBite — AI-Powered Food Safety and Dietary Assistant

A user-centered AI concept that helps people evaluate food products more safely by reading ingredient labels, recognizing allergens and dietary concerns, and responding with concise safety guidance.

Back to Artifacts Jump to Evidence

Introduction

SafeBite is a practical AI concept and prototype designed to help users make faster and safer food decisions. The project focuses on interpreting food packaging information such as ingredient labels and nutrition facts, then using that information to help users determine whether a product fits their dietary needs.

Description

The project was designed as an AI assistant that can identify common allergens, evaluate whether foods may be gluten-free or lactose-free, assess whether products appear organic, and comment on sugar-level concerns. It was also structured to remember relevant user dietary preferences during conversation and respond with concise, safety-first guidance.

Objective

The objective of SafeBite was to create a user-centered AI assistant that could support real-world shopping decisions by making food label interpretation faster, clearer, and more personalized.

Tools and Technologies Used

  • ChatGPT / custom AI prompt design
  • Prompt engineering and structured bot instructions
  • Food label interpretation logic
  • Image-based input analysis for packaging labels
  • Documentation and academic project writing

Process

I began by defining the user problem: many people have allergies, intolerances, or dietary restrictions but struggle to interpret food labels quickly. From there, I refined the assistant’s purpose, constraints, and response structure. I developed detailed bot instructions, improved the classification logic so that “safe” or “not safe” appears first, added user-memory behavior for dietary concerns, and strengthened the system so that explanations remain short, practical, and actionable. I also considered how the tool should interpret images of labels and how it should provide safer alternatives when a product does not fit the user’s needs.

Value Proposition

SafeBite demonstrates my ability to design an AI solution around a clear user need. It shows that I can move from an abstract idea to a structured, implementable system by defining requirements, building decision logic, and aligning outputs with user safety and usability.

Unique Value

What makes this project distinctive is its combination of personalization, safety-first communication, and real-life usefulness. Rather than being a generic food information tool, SafeBite was designed to support individual dietary concerns in a way that is simple enough for everyday use.

Relevance

This artifact is relevant because it reflects important professional skills: user-centered design, AI application, critical thinking, structured communication, and the ability to turn a common everyday problem into a practical digital solution. It also aligns with my broader interest in using AI to make information more accessible and decisions more informed.
